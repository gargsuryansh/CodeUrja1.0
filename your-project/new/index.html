Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ section for Digibhoomi (Land Management System) is designed to provide comprehensive answers to common queries related to our land management software. This section aims to assist users in understanding the functionality, benefits, and integration aspects of Digibhoomi, ensuring they can effectively utilize the system to meet their land management needs.

What is a Land Management System (Digibhoomi)?

A Land Management System is a digital solution designed to help organizations manage, monitor, and optimize the use of land assets. It integrates tools for land administration, mapping, property management, leasing, and legal compliance, facilitating efficient land use.

What are the Key Features of a Land Management System?

Key features of Digibhoomi typically include:

  • Land parcel mapping (GIS integration)
  • Land ownership and tenure records
  • Lease and contract management
  • Encroachment detection
  • Compliance and regulatory tracking
  • Document management for property records
  • Workflow automation for land transactions
  • Reporting and analytics

How Does an Digibhoomi Integrate with GIS?

Digibhoomi integrates Geographic Information Systems (GIS) to provide visual representations of land parcels, boundaries, and other spatial data. This enables users to view, edit, and analyze maps directly within the system, offering better land-use insights and decision-making.

Can Digibhoomi Help with Lease Management?

Yes, Digibhoomi can manage lease agreements, renewals, payments, and other lease-related information. It automates lease tracking, alerts for renewal deadlines, and ensures compliance with lease terms, making lease management more efficient.
