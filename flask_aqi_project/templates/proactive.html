Proactive Measures for Different AQI Levels

Good
0 - 50

Air quality is considered safe.

  • Measures
  • Enjoy outdoor activities freely.
  • Keep indoor plants to maintain freshness.
  • Encourage use of bicycles and public transport.
Moderate
51 - 100

Air quality is acceptable but sensitive individuals should take precautions.

  • Measures
  • Reduce prolonged outdoor activities if sensitive to air pollution.
  • Use air purifiers indoors.
  • Keep windows closed during peak pollution hours.
Unhealthy
101 - 150

Sensitive groups may experience health effects.

  • Measures
  • Avoid outdoor exercises and strenuous activities.
  • Wear N95 masks when stepping outside.
  • Use humidifiers to reduce pollutants indoors.
Very Unhealthy
151 - 200

Health warnings for the entire population.

  • Measures
  • Limit outdoor exposure as much as possible.
  • Keep windows and doors closed to prevent pollution from entering.
  • Increase indoor plants like Aloe Vera to improve air quality.
Hazardous
201+

Serious health risks for everyone.

  • Measures
  • Stay indoors with air purifiers running.
  • Use wet mopping instead of dry dusting.
  • Wear anti-pollution masks and avoid going out.
